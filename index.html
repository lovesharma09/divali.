Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights,
is one of the most widely celebrated festivals
in India and among Indian communities around the
world. This auspicious festival signifies the victory
of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over
ignorance. As homes are decorated with diyas, rangolis, and
vibrant lights, families come togetherto celebrate the joy,
love, and positivity that Diwali brings.
